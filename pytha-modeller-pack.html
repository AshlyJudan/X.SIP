Pytha Modeller Pack (Advanced)

The Pytha Modeller Pack (Advanced) is our most comprehensive training program, designed for aspiring professionals who aim to master 3D CAD modeling, rendering, and production planning with the powerful PYTHA software. This course provides an in-depth understanding of PYTHA's advanced functionalities, preparing you for complex design and manufacturing challenges.

Course Modules:

  • Understanding UI: Navigate and customize the PYTHA user interface efficiently.
  • 3D Modelling: Learn fundamental and advanced techniques for creating intricate 3D models.
  • Advanced 3D Modelling: Explore complex modeling tools and methodologies for sophisticated designs.
  • Library & Parametrics: Utilize and create parametric libraries for efficient and reusable components.
  • Model Rendering: Produce high-quality, photorealistic renderings of your designs.
  • Parts List: Generate accurate and detailed parts lists for manufacturing.
  • Quotations: Learn to create precise quotations directly from your models.
  • 2D drawing: Develop detailed 2D production drawings from 3D models.
  • Bore Holes: Master the creation and management of bore holes for joinery and hardware.
  • Technical Details: Understand and apply technical specifications and annotations.
  • Optimisation and Labelling: Optimize material usage and generate labels for efficient production.
  • Cam Interface: Integrate with CAM systems for automated manufacturing processes.
  • Beam Saw Integration: Prepare models for direct output to beam saw machines.
  • CNC Machine Integration: Seamlessly connect your designs to CNC machinery for automated fabrication.

This pack is ideal for those looking to achieve a high level of proficiency in PYTHA, enabling them to handle all aspects of design, presentation, and production planning.
